# Access Review Checklist  
Burn and Churn Coffee  
Quarter: Q1 2026

## Review Objectives
- Confirm active users still require assigned access
- Verify access aligns with job responsibilities
- Identify excessive privileges
- Identify inactive or terminated accounts
- Review vendor and administrative access
- Document findings and remediation actions

## Systems Reviewed
- Google Workspace
- Toast POS
- xtraCHEF
- QuickBooks Online
- Network Administration

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Confirm access reports were collected for all in-scope systems
- [ ] Confirm all listed users are still active or authorized
- [ ] Review privileged accounts for business need
- [ ] Review vendor accounts for current necessity
- [ ] Identify terminated users with active access
- [ ] Identify accounts requiring permission reduction
- [ ] Document findings
- [ ] Record remediation actions
- [ ] Obtain management review and approval

This checklist supports the quarterly access certification process for Burn and Churn Coffee and is used to document the review steps performed for Q1 2026.
